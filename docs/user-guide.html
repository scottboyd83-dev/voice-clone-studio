01 Voices

Read the 90 scripted prompts — each take passes a live quality gate (clipping, noise, level). Aim for 10–15 minutes of kept takes, then hit Build dataset.

03 Train

Fine-tune overnight

Pick your dataset, start the run, go to bed. Training is CPU-based (~3–5 hours for a 10-minute dataset). A ★ fine-tuned voice appears in the library when it's done.

05 Convert

Perform → any voice

The voice changer. Record or upload any speech (up to 5 min) and re-render it in a library voice. Your wording, pacing and emotion carry through — only the timbre is swapped. Singing mode keeps the melody.

Tip — when Generate gives the right words but the wrong read, perform the line yourself here instead. You direct; the model only swaps the voice.
06 History

Every take, reproducible

Each generation is stored with its exact settings and seed. Replay, download wav/mp3, filter by voice, or pull a seed back into Generate to make a result repeatable.

"Phase four is live." · ★ Scott (fine-tuned) · 3.1s
Tip — nothing leaves the machine. Deleting a voice removes its generations too.

First-run timing

Engines load lazily: first generation ~30s, first conversion ~1 min. After that, seconds. One-time model downloads happen on first ever use.

Three engines, one library

Instant clones (F5-TTS), fine-tuned clones (GPT-SoVITS), and the voice changer (Seed-VC) all share the same voice library.

Start it up

./run.sh then open localhost:5173. One-time engine setups: scripts/setup_gptsovits.sh, scripts/setup_seedvc.sh.
